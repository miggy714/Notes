Objective(Maximize): 
\\
\begin{align*}
 \text{Bending stiffnes per mass of material} &= \frac{EI}{m}; \text{    Where }m = \rho V
 \\
   &=\frac{EI}{\rho V}
    \\\text{This results in a material index M} &= \frac{E}{\rho}
    \\
    \log(M)&=\log(E) - log(\rho)
    \\
    \text{Material index loci in the form of: }  \log(E) &= \log(\rho) +\log(M)
\end{align*}
